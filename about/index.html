About

Well hey there!
Welcome.

Thanks for delving far enough into my website to find out a little more about me... In short, I'm a soul/pop artist from London. I was lucky enough to be born into a musical family and because of that, I've been singing for as long as I can remember. I started gigging professionally at just 14 years old and since then I've performed all over the country in rock bands, soul bands, jazz bands, and everything in between!
I'm now focusing on my solo project and I would love for you to show your support by signing up to my mailing list so I can update you on my music, and by checking out my stuff at the links below!
